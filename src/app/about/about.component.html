ABOUT US

For four decades, Maruthi Ceramics has been transforming homes and creating a culture of healthy, refined living. We curate the world’s most luxurious ceramics brands and offer them under one roof. With service that deeply respects every customer. At Maruthi you can choose from the world’s leading brands – Toto, Grohe, Hansgrohe, Jaquar, Hindware, Kajaria, Somany, Nitco, Rak, Franke, Carysil etc. Complementing this excellent choice is of course, excellent service – a Maruthi hallmark. In 2004, brothers Yathish and Naveen were handed the reins and legacy of the company. Their entrepreneurial vision drove the company to a whole new level, making Maruthi one of the largest distributors of the world’s leading ceramics brands. From one showroom it grew to three with a total space of 50,000 sq. ft. Maruthi Ceramics began to win over new generations of fans by the sheer breadth of premium products and the quality of service. Maruthi Ceramics is committed to providing comfort; enabling healthy and refined living; respecting the environment; and building life-enriching relationships.>

MISSION To become the most trusted name in luxury home solutions by – Offering high quality products from the world’s leading ceramics brands. Ensuring customer delight through impeccable service.
